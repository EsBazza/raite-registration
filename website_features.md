# RAITE 2026 — Comprehensive Chatbot Context Reference

This document is the official source of truth for the RAITE 2026 website and event, organized by PSITE Central Luzon (Region III).

---

## 1. WEBSITE NAVIGATION & USAGE

*   **Home Page (`/`)**: Hub for the event countdown, theme ("CTRL + NEXT"), and overall rankings from RAITE 2025.
*   **Competitions Page (`/competitions`)**: Catalog of all 12 events. Users can search and filter by category (Technical, Creative, Gaming).
*   **Profile Completion**: After signing up, users MUST complete their profile to unlock features.
    *   **Classification**: Users choose between **Participant** (Student) or **Faculty Coach**.
*   **Registration Process**: 
    *   Initiated only by **Faculty Coaches** via the "Join" button on a competition.
    *   **4-Step Flow**: 1. Select Event, 2. Team Information, 3. Upload Required Documents, 4. Final Review.
*   **My Registrations (`/registrations/my`)**: Dashboard for coaches to track approval status (Pending/Approved/Rejected) and submit project URLs for online competitions.
*   **Bulk Participant Registration**: Coaches can upload a CSV to register multiple students simultaneously.

## 4. COMPETITIONS & SPECIFIC RULES

1.  **IT Specialist**: Exam-based certification; highest score wins. Requires GMETRIX/Certiport.
2.  **Quiz Bee Challenge**: Onsite Sept 4; covers ICT trends, DBMS, cybersecurity.
3.  **CodeChum Programming**: Competitive coding; full-screen mode enforced.
4.  **Hackathon**: Team of 3; build/deploy simple app; BYOD.
5.  **Networking Challenge (2S1F)**: Technical mechanics provided in group chat.
6.  **Lanyard Design**: Deadline **Aug 9**; **Strictly NO AI**; submit PNG + Raw.
7.  **Infographics**: Deadline **Aug 9**; **Strictly NO AI**; 1080x1920px.
8.  **Micro Short Film**: 3-5 mins; original; **Strictly NO AI**; deadline Aug 9.
9.  **TechTok Challenge**: 30-90s vertical video; **Strictly NO AI**.
10. **E-Sports (ML/Valo)**: Double-elimination; **REQUIRED**: specific cross-arm/creative photo poses.
11. **Dance (Asian Pop)**: 8-15 members; 5-7 mins; Asian Pop music only.
12. **Mr. & Ms. Ambassador**: Pageant for students aged 17-27; **Strictly NO AI/Filters** on studio photos.

## 5. GENERAL POLICIES

*   **AI Policy**: **STRICTLY PROHIBITED** across all creative, technical, and programming challenges. Violation leads to automatic disqualification.
*   **One Competition Rule**: Each competitor may join only **ONE (1)** competition.
*   **Communication**: All participants are **REQUIRED** to join the official FB Messenger Group for their event.
*   **Scoring**: Champion (10 pts), 1st RU (7 pts), 2nd RU (4 pts), Participation (1 pt).
